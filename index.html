About Us

The Science of Pure Water

Reverse osmosis (RO) is a state-of-the-art water purification process that uses a semi-permeable membrane to separate harmful molecules from your drinking water. By applying pressure to overcome natural osmotic pressure, we guarantee unparalleled purity.

Capable of removing dissolved chemicals, heavy metals, and biological substances (principally bacteria), RO is the gold standard for producing safe, potable water. Trust Filter Traders to remove impurities and deliver health to your glass.
