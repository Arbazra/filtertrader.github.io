The Science of Clarity

Reverse osmosis (RO) relies on a high-pressure differential to push water molecules through a semi-permeable membrane at the microscopic level.

This strict physical barrier effectively isolates dissolved solids, harsh chemical species, and biological contaminants. The result is structural purity that meets rigorous industrial and domestic safety standards.
